Latitude vs. Max Temperature

As expected the weather becomes significanlty warmer as cities are located closer to the equator (0 Deg Latitude). More interestingly, however, is he fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth atthe time ofthe year this data was gathered.
